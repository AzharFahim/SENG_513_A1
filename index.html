Top 5 Football Stadium in the World

The magic and spirit of the beautiful game transcends age and time. 

Stories of the greatest athletes, the most memorable games, and the most amazing environments are told and passed down through out the centuries.

However, without the mentioned great players, their individual teams, and their followers, this would not be possible. Yet, without a stadium to house and capture all that lingers in our memory, the simple recollections would be lost. permanently lost.

In light of this, we will list the Top 5 football stadiums in the globe. I'll rate them according to the size of the stadium, the atmosphere, and the aesthetic appeal to help me decide where they should go.

The top 5 Football stadium in the world is:

  1. Wembley
  2. Camp Nou
  3. Santiago Bernabeu
  4. Old Trafford
  5. The Maracana
